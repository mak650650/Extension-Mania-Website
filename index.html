About Us

Extension Mania 2023 is the ultimate event for extension enthusiasts who want to showcase their skills and take their development to the next level. Held at IIT Madras, participants can compete individually or in teams and take part in various rounds, including a pre-event bootcamp, ideation round, and coding round. Expert mentors and like-minded developers will be available for networking opportunities throughout the event. Join us for an exhilarating experience and the chance to showcase your skills.

Registration

Register online for the event before May 25th, 2023 to secure your spot. For those who miss the deadline, don't worry, you can still register offline on the day of the event.

Bootcamp Day 1 (May 13th)

Join us for an exciting day of learning and development! Our expert trainers will introduce you to Extension development, cover basic concepts and tools required, and guide you through hands-on exercises to get started with extension development.

Bootcamp Day 2 (May 14th)

Get ready to level up your skills! Our trainers will cover advanced concepts and best practices in extension development, and guide you through hands-on exercises to implement various features and functionalities in extensions.

Extension Mania Day 1 (May 25th)

This is where the real challenge begins! The coding round for extension development will take place for 6 hours. All teams will be judged and the top 10 teams will be selected for the presentation round.

Extension Mania Day 2 (May 26th)

The top 10 teams will present their extensions. This is your chance to showcase your skills in front of industry experts and potential employers.

FAQ

What is Extension Mania?

A two-day event for developers, designers, and entrepreneurs interested in building and promoting their own browser extensions.

When will Extension Mania take place?

May 25-26, 2023, on the campus of IITM.

Where will the boot camp and Extension Mania event take place?

The boot camp will be held online, while the Extension Mania event will take place on the campus of IITM.

What kind of workshops will be available at Extension Mania?

Hands-on workshops covering various topics related to browser extensions.

Can I participate in Extension Mania if I don't have any experience with extensions?

Yes, beginners are welcome and resources will be provided.

What programming languages are allowed for building extensions?

A wide range of languages are welcome, including JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and more.

How many rounds will there be in Extension Mania?

Two rounds: a coding round and a presentation round.

What is the maximum team size for Extension Mania?

Teams can have up to 4 members.

What if I don't have a team for Extension Mania?

You can still register and we will help you find a team.

What are the prizes for Extension Mania?

Top 5 teams will receive a cash prize of 7,000 INR, coupons, and other exciting prizes. All participants will receive a certificate of participation.

How will our extension be judged at Extension Mania?

Extensions at Extension Mania will be judged based on various criteria, including functionality, creativity, usability, and overall impact.
